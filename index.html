Welcome to the WiV Virtual Assistant Throwdown

Our Test

Can your favorite voice assistant count by 2s or add two items to your shopping list at once? We tested the core capabilities of several major VAs and got some pretty hilarious responses like

Q: Count to 10 by twos

A: How long should the timer be?

or

Q: Is it Tuesday or Wednesday

A: No, Wednesday is not Tuesday. Tuesday is on July 21, 2020.

Not the most helpful response if you’ve lost track of the days while sequestered at home. We asked 189 questions of 5 major VAs and found surprisingly poor results, even on very simple questions. That was a bit sad but the testing was so fun that we wanted to get more people involved! Our test questions and the assessment guidelines are available at the Github repository Virtual Assistant-Tech and we want to invite Women in Voice to look at what we’ve done and use it as a jumping off point to probe YOUR favorite “smart” assistant AND WIN PRIZES.

The contest: Win a Nest Hub!

(sponsored by Conversational Technologies)

Email your best (aka worst), funniest examples to virtual-assistant-tech@googlegroups.com. Send your submissions at any time--you don’t have to wait for the sessions!

Winners will be announced Dec 4, and you don’t have to be a member of WiV to enter. Additional honorable mentions will also be awarded -- no prize other than great collective admiration.

Questions must be:

  • Reasonable (i.e. a virtual assistant could reasonably expected to answer them)
  • Free from speech recognition errors
  • In good taste
  • Verifiable by the judges (which means they have to be in English-sorry!)
  • In case of duplicates, the earliest entry takes precedence.
  • Judging criteria: humor, degree of misunderstanding (more misunderstanding is better), reasonableness of question.
  • Deadline: December 1 or when 500 entries have been received
  • Number of entries per person: 3

Format for submission:

  1. Q:
  2. A:
  3. Comments: for example, any relevant contextual information
  4. Assistant name:
