Welcome to the Digital Assistant Throwdown

Our Test

Can your favorite voice assistant count by 2s or add two items to your shopping list at once? We tested the core capabilities of several major VAs and got some pretty hilarious responses like

Q: Count to 10 by twos

A: How long should the timer be?

or

Q: Is it Tuesday or Wednesday

A: No, Wednesday is not Tuesday. Tuesday is on July 21, 2020.

Not the most helpful response if you’ve lost track of the days while sequestered at home. We asked 189 questions of 5 major VAs and found surprisingly poor results, even on very simple questions. That was a bit sad but the testing was so fun that we wanted to get more people involved! Our test questions and the assessment guidelines are available at the Github repository Virtual Assistant-Tech. We want to continue looking at what we’ve done and use it as a jumping off point to probe YOUR favorite “smart” assistant.

Join us!

We plan to have a monthly get-together to try things out and see how the assistants do. Sign up on Eventbrite.

Everyone is welcome, whether you’ve done any advance exploration or not--we think it will be especially fun for people to be able to test live during our conversation, so keep your favorite smart speaker or phone app version of same handy.

We also are collecting a corpus of interesting and challenging questions for virtual assistants. You can send anything you'd like to contribute to this Google Group and we'll add it to the Github corpus.

Contributions should be:

  • Reasonable (i.e. a virtual assistant could reasonably expected to answer them)
  • Free from speech recognition errors
  • In good taste
  • Verifiable (which means they have to be in English-sorry!)

Format for submission:

  1. Q:
  2. A:
  3. Comments: for example, any relevant contextual information such as the previous utterance.
  4. Assistant name:
